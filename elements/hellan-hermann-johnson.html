Hellan–Herrmann–Johnson

Click here to read what the information on this page means.

Orders\(0\leqslant k\)
Reference elementstriangle
Polynomial set\(\mathcal{Z}^{(0)}_{k}\)
↓ Show polynomial set definitions ↓↑ Hide polynomial set definitions ↑
\(\mathcal{Z}^{(0)}_k=\left\{\mathbf{M}\in\mathcal{P}_{k}^{d\times d}\middle|\mathbf{M}^t=\mathbf{M}\right\}\)

\(\mathcal{P}_k=\operatorname{span}\left\{\prod_{i=1}^dx_i^{p_i}\middle|\sum_{i=1}^dp_i\leqslant k\right\}\)
DOFsOn each edge: integral moments of inner products of normal to edge with an order \(k\) Lagrange space
On each face: point evaluations of tensor products with symmetric matrices whose entries are in (lagrange,k-1)
Number of DOFstriangle: \(3(k+1)(k+2)/2\) (A045943)
CategoriesMatrix-valued elements
